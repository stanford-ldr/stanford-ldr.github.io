Grigoris is a Research Scientist at Google Research in Mountain View. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Yale University, where his advisor was Amin Karbasi. His research focuses on learning theory and mechanism design.

Before Yale, he completed his undergraduate studies at NTUA (National Technical University of Athens) in computer science and electrical engineering, where he worked with Dimitris Fotakis.
