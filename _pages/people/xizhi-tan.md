Xizhi is a Motwani Postdoctoral Fellow at Stanford University, hosted by Aviad Rubinstein, Amin Saberi, and Ellen Vitercik. He is also affiliated with Stanford HAI. His research interests lie at the intersection of Economics and Computer Science, with a focus on algorithmic game theory, mechanism design, and fair division.

He completed his PhD in Computer Science at Drexel University, where he was a member of the Drexel EconCS research group and was advised by Vasilis Gkatzelis. He was a Student Researcher at Google Research in 2024 and a Research Intern at Google Research in 2023.
