Senem is a first-year PhD student at Stanford University, supported by the Stanford Management Science & Engineering fellowship. Her first-year advisors are Amin Saberi and Ellen Vitercik. Previously, she completed her Bachelor's with Honors in Mathematics and (concurrent) Master's in Computer Science (Theory & AI track) at Stanford.

She is broadly interested in theoretical and algorithmic foundations of learning and decision-making systems shaped by economic and societal forces.
