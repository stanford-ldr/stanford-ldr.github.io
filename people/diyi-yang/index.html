Diyi Yang

Assistant Professor, Computer Science

Stanford University

Webpage
Back to all people

Diyi is an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at Stanford, affiliated with the Stanford NLP Group, Stanford HCI Group, Stanford AI Lab (SAIL), and Stanford Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI). She is interested in Socially Aware Natural Language Processing. Her research goal is to better understand human communication in social context and build socially aware language technologies to support human-human and human-computer interaction.
